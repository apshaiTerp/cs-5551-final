    Details

    A new Cosmic Age is dawning and with it arrives a new cast of extraterrestrial beings struggling for complete galactic domination. How will these fresh faces affect the delicate cosmic balance of power?Only time will tell.Cosmic Incursion brings 20 additional races to the core set and marks the return of 10 classic Cosmic Encounter aliens. Players will face the sticky terror of the Fungus, scratch their heads at the perplexing Magician and shudder in fear as the Locust consumes their planets in one gulp.We've expanded the universe and added components for a 6th player to the game. In addition, Cosmic Incursion introduces a new variant to spice up gameplay the reward deck, which offers exciting new cards such as kickers and rifts to those aliens who ally with the defense.Cosmic Incursion requires the Cosmic Encounter board game to play.
